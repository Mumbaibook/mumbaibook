Rules & Regulations

Game Rules

  • For Game1, Game2 and Game6 5 numbers among them will be declared as winning numbers.
  • For Game1, Game2 and Game6 you will get 20X profits
  • For Game3, Game4 and Game5 5 numbers among them will be declared as winning numbers.
  • For Game3, Game4 and Game5 you will get 4X profits
  • Daily In any game for one number u will get 5 Super winning numbers.
  • For each Super winning number u will get 10X More profits.
  • You can play any number of bets.

General Betting Rules

  • All bets are final once confirmed and cannot be canceled or changed.
  • Minimum bet amount is ₹10 and maximum bet amount is ₹10,000 per bet.
  • Users must be 18 years or older to place bets.
  • Any attempt to manipulate the system will result in account suspension.
  • Winnings are calculated based on the odds at the time the bet was placed.
  • In case of any dispute, Mumbai Book's decision will be final.
  • Bets will be settled based on the official results only.

Deposit Rules

  • Minimum deposit amount is ₹100.
  • Deposits must be made from a payment method registered in your name.
  • All deposits must be verified before the amount is credited to your account.
  • Deposits may take up to 30 minutes to reflect in your account during peak times.
  • You must provide correct UTR/Transaction ID for deposit verification.
  • Deposits made through third-party accounts will be rejected.
  • Any deposit made using fraudulent methods will be reversed and account may be suspended.

Withdrawal Rules

  • Minimum withdrawal amount is ₹500.
  • Maximum withdrawal amount is ₹50,000 per day.
  • Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours on business days.
  • 2% processing fee will be deducted from all withdrawals.
  • You can only withdraw to bank accounts registered in your name.
  • Withdrawal requests may require additional verification.
  • You must have placed at least one bet before withdrawing deposited funds.
  • Withdrawals may be delayed during security checks.

Account Rules

  • One account per person is allowed. Multiple accounts will be suspended.
  • You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure.
  • Mumbai Book will never ask for your password.
  • Inactive accounts (no login for 6 months) may be deactivated.
  • You must provide accurate personal information during registration.
  • Accounts may be suspended for suspicious activity.
Important Note: These rules are subject to change without prior notice. It is your responsibility to check this page regularly for updates. By using our services, you agree to comply with all our rules and regulations.
