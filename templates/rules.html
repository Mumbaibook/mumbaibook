Game Rules

Game Schedule

Morning Game (00-99) Profit: 20x
Afternoon Game (00-49) Profit: 10x
Evening Game (00-19) Profit: 4x
Players can select up to 5 numbers in each game session.
Minimum bet amount is ₹10 per number.
Winnings are calculated based on the multiplier for each game type.
Results are announced immediately after each game session ends.

Deposit & Withdrawal Rules

Minimum deposit amount is ₹100.
Minimum withdrawal amount is ₹500.
Withdrawal requests are processed within 24 hours.
Bank account details must match the registered user's information.

General Rules

Players must be 18 years or older to participate.
Multiple accounts from the same user are not allowed.
The company reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate the rules.
All transactions and game results are final.
Important Note: Please gamble responsibly. If you feel you have a gambling problem, please seek help and contact our support team.
